Reuben Sandwich II

Description

Packed with flavor, these Rueben sandwiches are the perfect quick and easy meal for a busy fall night. Recipe creator COLETTE G. likes to serve the sandwiches with dill pickles and a bowl of vegetable soup. Many reviewers like to use a George Foreman grill to make these sandwiches so it eliminates the need for flipping, which can get messy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
    • Â½ cup Thousand Island dressing

Directions

  1. Preheat a large skillet or griddle on medium heat.
  2. Lightly butter one side of bread slices. Spread non-buttered sides with Thousand Island dressing. On 4 bread slices, layer 1 slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut and second slice of Swiss cheese. Top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides out.
  3. Grill sandwiches until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
